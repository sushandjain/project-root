About Kheti Bazaar

Kheti Bazaar is a revolutionary web-based platform designed to eliminate middlemen and empower farmers by providing direct access to buyers. Our goal is to create a fair and transparent marketplace that benefits both farmers and consumers. By connecting farmers directly with buyers, we ensure better prices for farmers and fresher produce for consumers.
