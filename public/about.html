Our Mission

Kheti Bazaar was founded with a clear vision: to transform agricultural commerce by creating a direct connection between farmers and buyers. We believe that by eliminating intermediaries, we can ensure that farmers receive fair compensation for their hard work while buyers gain access to fresh, high-quality produce at reasonable prices.

Our platform is designed to be simple yet powerful, focusing on the needs of both farmers and consumers. By leveraging modern technology, we're making it easier than ever for farmers to showcase their products and for buyers to find exactly what they're looking for, all while supporting local agriculture and sustainable farming practices.

What We Offer

Easy Registration

Simple sign-up process for both farmers and buyers with streamlined profile creation that takes just minutes to complete.

Product Listings

Farmers can easily upload their products with detailed descriptions, quantities, and availability information.

Location-Based Search

Find nearby farmers using our geolocation feature, making it easier to source local produce without traveling far.

Direct Messaging

Communicate directly with farmers or buyers through our built-in messaging system to discuss products, prices, and pickups.

Fair Pricing

By eliminating middlemen, we ensure farmers get better returns while buyers pay fair prices for fresh products.

Mobile Friendly

Access our platform from anywhere using any device with our responsive web design optimized for mobile use.

Future Enhancements

Payment Processing Integration

We're working on implementing secure payment gateways to enable online transactions directly on our platform.

Advanced Search & Filtering

Soon you'll be able to find products using advanced filters for price, category, ratings, and availability.

Delivery & Logistics Services

Future updates will include partnerships with delivery services to streamline transportation of goods from farmers to buyers.

Data Analytics & AI Recommendations

We plan to implement analytics to help farmers track demand trends and AI-powered recommendations for buyers based on preferences.

Multi-Language Support

To make our platform more accessible, we're working on adding support for multiple languages.
